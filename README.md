## How it works?

1. **Pitch In** — Got a spark of inspiration? Post it under the **Ideas** section or participate in our **Polls**. Even a brief thought can make a huge difference.
2. **Boost Your Favorites.** Use your upvotes! The board is dynamically sorted, pushing the community's top priorities straight to our radar.
3. **Watch It Grow.** Our development is driven entirely by you. Because we are crafting this Debian-based OS from the ground up, your feedback directly dictates what we code next.

## Categories

* 💡 **Ideas** — Submit and upvote new features or system tools.
* 🗳️ **Polls** — Help us make crucial design and technical decisions.
* 🙌 **Showcase** — Flex your custom AYAS OS ricing, themes, and personalized desktop layouts.
* 🙏 **Q&A** — Seek guidance or lend a hand to fellow users.

## Guidelines

* Keep the environment friendly, constructive, and respectful towards everyone (including users of other operating systems).
* Use the search bar before creating a new thread to keep things tidy. If someone beat you to it, just give it an upvote!
* Reserve this space strictly for innovation and discussion. Technical bugs belong in the specific repository's issue tracker.
